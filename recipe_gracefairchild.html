Chocolate Eclair Dessert

With layers of graham cracker and fluffy vanilla pudding, this quick and easy no bake dessert is delicious. Tastes best if it sits overnight in the fridge.

Ingredients

  • 2 individual packages of graham crackers
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages of instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 3 cups of milk
  • 1 (8 ounce) container of frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 1 (16 ounce) package of prepared chocolate frosting

Directions

  1. Line the bottom of a 9x13-inch pan with graham crackers
  2. In a large bowl, combine the pudding mix and milk, stir well
  3. Mix the whipped topping into the pudding mixture
  4. Spread half of the mixture over the first graham cracker layer
  5. Top with another layer of graham crackers and remaining pudding
  6. Top it all with a final layer of graham crackers
  7. Frost with the chocolate icing
  8. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving to allow graham crackers to soften
Prep Time: 15 minutes, Refrigerate Time: 2 hours
